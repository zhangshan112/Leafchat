LeafChat is the social network for plant lovers — discover, share, and grow together.

Whether you are nurturing your first pothos or curating a balcony jungle, LeafChat connects you with a vibrant community of growers who understand your obsession.

YOUR GREEN JOURNEY STARTS HERE

• Share photos and updates of your plants with the community
• Browse a personalized home feed organized by garden themes
• Like, comment, and save posts from growers around the world
• Tag plants in your posts and explore content by species
• Follow other plant lovers and build your own following

DISCOVER & LEARN

• Explore the Plant Encyclopedia with care guides, light and water tips, and plant profiles
• Identify plants from a photo using on-device AI (powered by Apple Intelligence on supported devices)
• Search for plants, users, and posts in one place
• Collect plants you love and track your personal collection

CONNECT WITH THE COMMUNITY

• Join themed garden spaces — from rescue stories to new growth and balcony inspiration
• Send direct messages and voice notes to other growers
• Get notified about likes, comments, follows, and new messages

PERSONALIZED FOR YOU

• Choose your favorite plants during onboarding for a tailored feed
• Build your profile, showcase your collection, and earn a Plus member badge

FREE TO JOIN

Create a free account and start sharing. Free members can publish posts and identify plants each month. Upgrade anytime for higher limits or unlimited access.

LEAFCHAT BASIC
Get more monthly posts and a larger plant identification quota. Available as a weekly or monthly auto-renewable subscription.

LEAFCHAT PLUS
Unlock unlimited posts, unlimited AI plant identifications, saved posts, premium garden themes, and a Plus member badge on your profile. Available as a weekly or monthly auto-renewable subscription.

IDENTIFICATION CREDITS
Need extra identifications? Purchase credit packs anytime without a subscription.
